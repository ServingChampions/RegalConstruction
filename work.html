Our Projects

Explore our featured projects where luxury meets precision and innovation blends with tradition. These exceptional creations reflect our commitment to bringing your dreams to life.

Anaheim Hills

Spanish inspired home nestled into the hills on an acre lot. New construction project with a remodeled hardscape to tie in the Spanish hacienda style.

Project Description:

  • Square Footage: 4,452sqft
  • Location: Anaheim Hills, CA
  • Specs: 4 Bedrooms, 3 ½ Baths, Office, Detached Casita, Garage, Pool and Sports Court
  • Design Team:
    • Architect - Jason Richart
    • Landscape Designer - David Pederson

Newport Coast

Discover the epitome of luxury living in the heart of Newport Coast—a mesmerizing fusion of interior and exterior spaces. Immerse yourself in the allure of a newly crafted open floorplan seamlessly connected to an innovative outdoor design, elevating the art of entertainment and redefining modern living.

Project Description:

  • Scope: Created an open floor plan design to integrate a seamless interior and exterior living space.

Laguna Beach - Coastal Contemporary

Coastal Contemporary gem sitting on the bluffs of Laguna Beach. Designed by the renowned Horst Noppenberger. This house is the epitome of luxury coastal living, with exposed Steal Beams, floor to ceiling windows and doors showcasing unobstructed ocean views from all three levels.

Project Description:

  • Square Footage: 2,972 sqft including an ADU
  • Location: Three Arch Bay, Laguna Beach, CA
  • Specs: 4 Bedrooms, 4 ½ Baths
  • Design Team:
    • Architect/Designer - Horst Noppenberger
    • Landscape Designer - M.D. Wilkes Design and Consulting
